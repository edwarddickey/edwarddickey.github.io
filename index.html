Technological Advances: Assistive technologies, such as screen readers and voice recognition software, have improved significantly, but their effectiveness relies on the accessibility of the websites themselves.
Cultural Differences: Attitudes toward disability and accessibility can vary by culture, impacting how accessibility is prioritized in different regions.
Economic Considerations: Businesses that prioritize accessibility often reach a broader audience, improving their market reach and customer satisfaction.
Ongoing Challenges: Despite progress, many websites remain inaccessible. Awareness, education, and enforcement of accessibility standards are essential to drive change.
